Our Team

Who We Are

We are a big diverse group of professional individuals who wish to share our talents by creating amazing knife sets for everyones liking.

Backstory

Hello I am Liam, the founder of Cutting Edge Blades alongside Bill the Co-founder. we came up with the idea for this company when we were in our early collage career in 2005. It started out small just me and Bill making knives out of wood and metal in our dads garage. we realised we could take this further so we created Cutting Edge Blades and went from there.

Goals

We here at Cutting Edge Blades hope to someday have our delivery service span to all places globally. we want people from all over the world to have access to our blades. We also hope to someday open up in person stores to allow customers to feel and hold our knives before they purchase them to ensure optimal comfort for the user.
